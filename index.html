The Care of Magical Creatures

A 6th Grade WebQuest Adventure

Welcome, Young Wizards!

Attention, 6th Grade students!

Professor Hagrid has lost his notebook! The Ministry of Magic is sending inspectors to Hogwarts next week to check if students know how to handle magical creatures safely.

We need your help. You must become Magizoologists (experts in magical creatures). Your mission is to research a specific magical creature and create a guide so the rest of the school stays safe.

Are you ready to open your books and cast some spells? Grab your wands!

Your Mission

Working in pairs, you will create a "Magical Creature Fact File" (poster or digital slide).

Your Fact File must be written entirely in English and include:

  • Name of the creature.
  • Appearance: What does it look like? (Use adjectives!).
  • Habitat: Where does it live?
  • Abilities: What magic can it do?
  • Danger Level: Is it safe or dangerous for Muggles?

At the end of the 3rd session, you will present your creature to the class to earn your "Creature Permit".

The Process (3 Sessions)

Session 1: The Library Search (Research)

  • Choose a partner.
  • Select one creature: Hippogriff, Niffler, Bowtruckle, Phoenix, or Thestral.
  • Use the links in the Resources section to find information.
  • Write down 5 key adjectives to describe your creature.

Session 2: The Quill & Ink (Creation)

  • Draft your text in English. Remember to use full sentences!
  • Example: "The Niffler is a small, black creature. It likes shiny things."
  • Create your poster or slide. Draw your creature or use an image.
  • Check your spelling (spells don't work if you pronounce them wrong!).

Session 3: The Great Hall (Presentation)

  • Present your creature to the class (2 minutes per pair).
  • Speak clearly and confidently.
  • Submit your link or poster to the professor.
