Ministry of Magic: Career Day

Unit: The World of Work (3rd & 4th Grade)

Welcome to the Ministry!

Attention! There is a mystery at the Ministry of Magic. Someone stole a Golden Snitch yesterday!

We need new Aurors (police), Journalists, and Engineers to help us.

In this WebQuest, you will learn about jobs and investigate what people were doing when the Snitch disappeared.

Your Magic Mission

1. The Job Profile

Pick a job from page 69. Describe their workplace and their uniform.

2. The Alibi (The Mystery)

Use the Past Continuous to say what your character was doing at 10:00 AM yesterday.

Example: "I was working in the laboratory."

3. The Truth Healer

Confirm information using Tag Questions.

Example: "You are a mechanic, aren't you?"

The 3 Sessions

Session 1: Vocabulary Search

Open your book at pages 69 and 70. Find 4 jobs and 4 places (factory, hospital, etc.).

Session 2: The Investigation (Page 72)

Imagine you are a witness. Write 3 sentences in your notebook:

  • "Yesterday at 10:00, I was wearing my uniform."
  • "I was working in the office."
  • "My partner was helping a patient."

Session 3: Final Poster

Create a poster with your "Ministry Job". Present it to the class and ask your friends 2 tag questions.

The Veritaserum Challenge

Can you solve the mystery?
